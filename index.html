Who are we ?

In normal times, while pursuing or after completion of graduation a number of students seek to gain industry oriented or job oriented knowledge which would help him/her build her career. There are a number of institutions providing excellent knowledge about industry and VLSI. But, due to the ensuing pendamic, the students are left with the only option of online platforms to learn and build themselves. So, here we come together to teach, mentor and help the interested students for VLSI industries and help them to gain adequate knowledge to shine in this field.
